Crystal Collector!

You will be given a target number at the start of this game. There are four crystals below. Each crystal is assigned a random number value.By clicking the crystal you will add points to your score.

You win the game by matching your score to the target number. If you go over the target number you lose. The number value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

At the start of every new game the crystals will be assigned a new random number.
